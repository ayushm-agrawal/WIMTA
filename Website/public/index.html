About WIMTA

WIMTA, is userfriendly creation to make student's life a lot easier.

It is a quick check-in and check-out app which enables students to get the availability of a TA and current checkedin checkout status.

It comes very handy everytime when student wonders wheather to go to src for help and whatif my TA is not there and I will have to come back. It also enables students to see the schedule of TA.

How it Works?

WIMTA stores authorised TA information in firebase database. When TA check into the app in Student Resource centre, it gets update on the database which is then displayed onto the apps and website. Checkin signals are sent to raspberry pi via bluetooth.
